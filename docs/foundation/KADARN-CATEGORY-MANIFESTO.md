# KADARN CATEGORY MANIFESTO

## The Birth of a New Category

---

For decades, the life sciences industry has invested billions of dollars in science.

Yet one critical infrastructure has never existed.

**The infrastructure that preserves institutional capability.**

Every year, thousands of hospitals, clinical research sites, laboratories, physician practices and biobanks generate extraordinary operational knowledge.

They recruit patients.

Collect biospecimens.

Execute complex protocols.

Maintain regulatory compliance.

Develop specialized capabilities.

Build trusted scientific relationships.

But when projects end...

Most of that institutional value becomes fragmented.

Hidden.

Locked inside sponsor databases.

Lost inside CRO networks.

Buried in documents.

Forgotten when organizations change partners.

The industry repeatedly rediscovers institutions that have already proven themselves.

This is not a technology problem.

**It is an infrastructure problem.**
