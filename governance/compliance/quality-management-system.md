# Kadarn Quality Management System
## ISO 20387 Alignment — Draft

### 1. Quality Objectives
- Specimen QC pass rate > 95%
- Temperature compliance > 99%
- Trust score accuracy within 0.05
- Audit trail completeness 100%

### 2. Quality Controls
- QC events tracked per specimen via Specimen Twin
- Temperature monitoring via Shipment Twin
- Trust score computation via Trust Engine
- Provenance Graph for full traceability
